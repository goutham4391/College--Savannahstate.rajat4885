Graduate Studies

SSU's location in the historic, international city of Savannah provides students with a rich urban experience and a wealth of contacts and professional connections during the course of their program.

Graduate programs prepare students for leadership positions in research, education, and professional careers.

SSU students take advantage of internship and employment opportunities, as well as opportunities to work with other students and our graduate faculty to create new knowledge that will benefit the present and the future.

SSU's Office of Graduate Studies is the point of contact for both prospective and current students. Our office offers academic resources, research, and assistantship opportunities for qualified graduate students.
