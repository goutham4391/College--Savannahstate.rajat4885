Next Steps

After being accepted into Savannah State University and the dual enrollment program please take the following steps:

Register For Classes

The SSU College Course Registration Liaison and your high school guidance counselor will work together to select college courses that will meet your high school graduation requirements. Click the button below for a list of dual enrollment approved courses.

Dual Enrollment Course List

Pay Fees

Tuition is free for all dual enrollment approved courses. The student is responsible for any lab or resource fees associated with those courses.

Student Financial Services

Choose Books

The bookstore will loan textbooks to student enrolled in the dual enrollment program each semester, depending on course availability. Textbooks must be returned to Savannah State University at the end of each semester. The student may be charged a lost or damaged book fee if the book is not returned in the required condition.
